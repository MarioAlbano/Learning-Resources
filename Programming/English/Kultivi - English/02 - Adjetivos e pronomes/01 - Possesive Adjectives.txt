Possessive adjectives


Ex: What's your name? / My name is paul.  
What's your nationality? / I am Brazilian.
What's your occupation? / I am a teacher.

My		Meu, minha (s)
Your		Teu, seu, sua (s)
His		Seu, sua (s), dele
Her		Seu, sua (s), dela
Its		Seu, Sua, (s), dele, dela
Our		Nosso, Nossa (s)
Your		Teus, tuas, seus, suas
Their	Deles, delas

White	yellow	blue
Gray	orange	red
Purple	green	black

What color is your house?
My house is white.
