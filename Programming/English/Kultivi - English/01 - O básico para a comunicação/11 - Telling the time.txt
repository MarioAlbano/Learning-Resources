
11 - Telling the time


What time is it now?
It's ____.

Full time = O'clock 			Ex: 2:00 = Two o'clock 
60 minutes = O'clock		15 minutes = A quarter		30 minutes = Half

Say pm or am only in future / past, not in present

Ex:
7:05 It's seven oh five / It's five past seven
7:10 It's seven and ten / It's seven tem / it's tem past seven
7:15 It's seven fifteen / It´s fifteen past seven / it's a quarter past seven 
7:25 It's seven twenty-five / it's twenty-five past seven. 
7:30 it's seven thirty / it's thirty past seven / it's Half past seven / 
7:40 It's seven forty / It's fifteen to eight / it's a quarter to eight / 
7:55 It's seven fifty-five / it's five to eight 
8:00 It's eight o'clock
